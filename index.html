The Road

Cormac McCarthy

The Road is a 2006 post-apocalyptic novel by American writer Cormac McCarthy. The book details the grueling journey of a father and his young son over a period of several months across a landscape blasted by an unspecified cataclysm.The book was adapted into a film of the same name in 2009, directed by John Hillcoat.
